Moon & Mercury Signs

Your moon sign is how you display and process your emotions. The moon sign is considered one of the most important placements alongside the sun and rising sign. They are more commonly known as the Big 3. Your mercury sign is how you communicate. Usually your mercury sign is the same as your sun sign but can sometimes be the sign before or after your sign. Some people can feel a bit disconnected from their sun sign if their mercury sign is different. For example, a Libra with a Libra Mercury will communicate differently from a Libra with a Scorpio Mercury.
